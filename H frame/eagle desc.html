You might have seen a flying eagle. Eagles are known as birds of prey because they hunt animals as their food. They are similar to hawks, falcons, and vultures. Due to their strength and soaring flight, eagles held a special place in the human imagination. From ancient times the golden eagle has been a symbol of power. The national symbol of the United States is the Bald Eagle. Eagles are present all over the world. They fly high in the sky.
